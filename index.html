    Project

    Competent Communicator

    CC1: The Ice Breaker

  • Discover your skills
  • Explore the stage
  • Introduce yourself
    •

    CC2: Organize your speech

  • Strong opening and conclusion
  • Write down your speech
  • Select structure
    •

    Structure: Chronological, Problem-solution, Hierarchical

    CC3: Get to the point

    What is your goal?

  • To inform
  • To persuade
  • To entertain
  • To inspire
    •

    CC4: How to say it

  • Select right and vivid words
  • Use rhetorical devices
  • Use correct grammar
    •

    Alliteration, Repetition, Metaphor, Hyperbole

    Hungry like a wolf

    I was so hungry, I could eat an elephant.

    CC5: Your body speaks

  • Stance, movement, gestures
  • Smooth and natural body language
  • Use facial expression and eye contact
    •

    CC6: Vocal Variety

    Experiment with voice:

  • Volume
  • Pitch
  • Rate
  • Quality
    •

    Pitch: High/Low. Monotone, Squeking

    Rate: Number of words

    Quality: Confidence

    CC7: Research your topic

  • Collect information
  • Support your points
    •

    Projector is allowed, but we prefer:

  • Artifacts
  • Flipcharts
  • Whiteboards
    •

  • Excite listeners about your topic
  • Convince listeners to change their opinion
  • Call listeners to action
